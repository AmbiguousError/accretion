Solar System Evolution Simulator

This is an N-body physics simulation that models how our solar system might have evolved differently based on the properties of Jupiter.

New in V3: Track the Outcomes

  • Collision Detection: The simulation now detects when asteroids hit a planet or the Sun.
  • "Fate of the Asteroids" Dashboard: A new panel tracks the results of your simulation in real-time, counting how many asteroids deliver water to Earth, get absorbed by giants, or are ejected from the system entirely.

Run different scenarios to see which one is best for creating a habitable, water-rich Earth!
