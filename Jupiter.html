The Rare Earth Simulator

This simulation explores the delicate conditions that make a planet like Earth habitable. How many things had to go right?

Features to Explore:

  • The Habitable Zone: A green ring shows the "Goldilocks Zone" where liquid water can exist.
  • Impact Clock: The dashboard tracks the years since Earth's last major asteroid impact, highlighting the need for stability.
  • The "Grand Tack": Activate this special scenario to see how Jupiter's ancient migration might have been essential for life. This scenario starts before the inner planets have formed, showing Jupiter clearing a path through a disk of smaller bodies.

Experiment with the controls to see how fragile a habitable world truly is.
